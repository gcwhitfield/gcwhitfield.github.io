Music

I've been very enthusiastic about my music studies for most of my life. I started taking piano lessons during fifth grade at The Real School of Music in Burlington, Massachusetts, where I mainly focused on classical music with Bill Doss until high school when I switched primarily to jazz under the instruction of Eric Goldberg.

In high school I started creating electronic music using Logic Pro X under the alias Poshon and posting it to SoundCloud and YouTube. I also wrote the soundtracks for two video games while working with the Game Creation Society at Carnegie Mellon during my freshman year.

I'm currently focusing on my jazz and electronic music studies, but I also write other music for fun too. I hope to release an electronic album on Spotify sometime in the future :).

I've attached a few samples of my work below. These include electronic pieces, orchestral pieces and piano performance recordings.
