DANGER DANCER

Fall 2018 - Music & Sound Effects

Overview

In the far future, conventional dance has become boring. To entertain the masses, a new form of extreme dancing is born: DANGER DANCE. Are you up to the challenge to become the best DANGER DANCER in the world?

This was the first game that I worked in with the Game Creation Society at CMU! For this project I was in charge of writing the music and creating sound effects. I met with other students each Sunday during the semester for about 3 hours. I was able to bounce ideas off of them and get feedback on how to improve the music and sounds. Having no idea what the final video game would look like made it a bit challenging to search for a musical style that would fit the game. Eventually, we decided the music needed to fit at least 3 criteria: it should be danceable, lazery, and funky.

If you're interested, you can also check out DANGER DANCER on the Game Creation Society website.
